The population

Why Silver River?

Florida's Silver River supports the only established free-ranging population of Macaca mulatta in North America outside managed facilities — a population introduced in the late 1930s that has persisted for nearly a century.

Despite their visibility to the thousands of kayakers and park visitors who encounter them annually, basic population parameters remain poorly documented. This project is building that record.

The gap

What we're measuring

~200 estimated individuals; precise count unknown
1938 first recorded introduction; no systematic monitoring since
0 peer-reviewed datasets on spatial distribution or abundance
2025 project start; first long-term observational dataset begins

The app

Silver Springs Macaque Monitor

A mobile app for systematic sighting reports and GPS transect surveys along the Silver River. No scientific background required — and a route with no sightings is as valuable as one with ten.
